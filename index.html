Hello, I'm in a production service. This mean that I have to prioritise the tasks everyday.

How to deal with it ?

For anything other than production incidents, please send me an e-mail instead sending me an instant message.

Images of a prod worker realizing he won't be able to answer everyone's questions

I am working with a sorting system based on priorities in my mailbox.

Organization

I read my mail two times a day (before lunch and before the end of working day).

To do my best and answer as quickly as possible I thank you to respect the following guidelines:

  • Add a prioritisation label on the mail
  • Describe the problem or the question with a maximum of context and details.

Priority levels

For the prioritisation label, you have a few choices:

  • High: Subject needed to be treated this day or next day.
  • Medium: Subject needed to be treated during current week or next week.
  • Low: Subject needed to be treated during current month.

It's important to respect these level of priorities. Otherwise you will get a place in the junk folders

It's a joke! But please respect it :)

To win some time you can generate a sample of the mail. You just have to select the priority level.
